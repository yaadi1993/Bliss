Message

The entire effort is dedicated to cultivate the wisdom necessary for "self-realization" and the "realization of the Ultimate Truth" behind this matrix of illusion. Gurbani, described as "A Beautiful Gift to Humanity," is a wellspring of profound wisdom. To facilitate deeper understanding, several references from the Guru Granth Sahib Ji have been thoughtfully grouped together.
